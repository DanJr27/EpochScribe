======================================================================== EPOCHSCRIBE v1.0 - Official Adventure Journal
Project Epoch (3.3.5 Client)

--- OVERVIEW --- EpochScribe is a lightweight, immersive note-taking tool built for the Project Epoch "Classic+" experience. It is designed to stay out of your way while you adventure, offering a quick-access tab to document discoveries, hidden world events, and rare spawns.

--- INSTALLATION ---

Extract the 'EpochScribe' folder into your game directory: [Your Game Folder]/Interface/AddOns/

Ensure the file path looks like this: Interface/AddOns/EpochScribe/EpochScribe.toc Interface/AddOns/EpochScribe/EpochScribe.lua

If the addon does not load, ensure "Load out of date Addons" is checked at the character selection screen.

--- CORE FEATURES ---

CHAT INTEGRATION: A new 'Notes' tab is added above your General Chat. Click it to toggle the journal instantly.
NEW NOTE BUTTON: Automatically inserts a clean header containing:
Current Server Time
Current Zone Name
Precise Coordinates
SMART KEYBOARD FOCUS: You can keep the window open while playing! Clicking the game world, the window border, or hitting ESC will release your keyboard so you can use WASD to move immediately.
FULLY RESIZABLE: Use the grabber icon in the bottom-right corner to scale the window to fit your UI perfectly.
CLOSE BUTTON: Standard [X] in the top right for quick closing.
--- COMMANDS ---

/scribe : Toggles the journal visibility.
--- DATA SAVING --- Notes are saved automatically per character. Your logs for your Mage will be separate from your logs for your Rogue. Data is stored locally in your 'WTF' folder.

--- TROUBLESHOOTING ---

Keyboard stuck in the text box? Click anywhere on the grass/ground in-game or on the border of the notepad to release it.
Window missing? Type /scribe or check your Addon list at login.
